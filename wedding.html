We’ll be tying the knot on Saturday, August 10th in sunny (well, we’re hoping) California. Here’s the low-down on the big day itself.

The Ceremony

Our wedding ceremony will take place in the chapel of Mills College, Oakland at 3pm.

Mills is an independent liberal arts and sciences college for women, where Amiee did her undergrad degree before embarking on her quest to Ireland in search of a man pursuit of her folklore degree. It's a beautiful wooded campus at the foot of the Oakland Hills, located nine miles south-east of the reception venue in Berkeley.

The Reception

After the ceremony, we will be having a reception at the Bancroft Hotel in Berkeley.

Located directly across from the UC Berkeley college campus, the Bancroft is a boutique 1920s style hotel in the heart of Berkeley.
